About Us

The club focuses on quantum computing, exploring the mathematical foundations and physical principles that define the next era of technology. We dive deep into the world of subatomic processing, where traditional bits are replaced by qubits that exist in multiple states simultaneously.

Our mission at NIT Jalandhar is to empower students with the tools of quantitative analysis and quantum logic. By blending high-level physics with advanced computer science, we aim to lead research in quantum algorithms, secure communication, and ultra-fast optimization.

Chi Chi Robot Dog

Chi Chi represents the pinnacle of our hardware initiatives. This quadrupedal robotic platform integrates real-time sensor fusion and computer vision, serving as a physical manifestation of the complex algorithms developed within the Super Quant Club.
